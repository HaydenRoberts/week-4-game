Crystal
Collector v1
Instructions:
1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
2. There are four crystals below. By clicking a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
3. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. Every 5 wins moves you up a level. 21 wins you beat the game but 10 losses you lose.
4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
5. Each time the game restarts, the values of the crystals change as well as the random number.
